# Postmortem Notes You Can Reuse
Date: 2026-01-08
Tags: reliability, incidents, writing
Summary: A compact template for incident writeups that engineers actually read.

A useful postmortem answers four questions: what failed, why we missed it, what we changed, and how we prove it stays fixed.
Keep sections short and concrete. Replace passive language with timestamps and decisions.
The best postmortem is not dramatic. It is specific enough to edit runbooks the same day.
