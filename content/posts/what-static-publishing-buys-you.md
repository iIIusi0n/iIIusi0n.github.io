# What Static Publishing Buys You
Date: 2026-01-26
Tags: docs, infra, performance
Summary: Why static files still win for docs, changelogs, and engineering blogs.

Static publishing removes a category of operational failures. No runtime query path means fewer things break at 2 AM.
The tradeoff is discipline: authors must keep content structured and explicit.
For engineering blogs and changelogs, that trade usually pays off immediately.
